About:

NCERT Class 9 Maths book covers all the basic and fundamental concepts included in class 9th Maths syllabus prescribed by CBSE. These textbooks make it easier for students to prepare effectively for their board examinations as well as several competitive exams like NTSE, KVPY, International Mathematical Olympiads IMO, and more.This latest NCERT book for Class 9 Maths explains all the concepts and theorems in the explanatory way which makes learning quite simple and effective.
