Book Name: BUSINESS STUDIES PRINCIPLES AND FUNCTIONS OF MANAGEMENT PART 1

Author: NCERT

About:

NCERT Books for Class 12 Business Studies is an excellent introduction to understand business fundamentals related to modern times. The textbook is a handy resource which helps students brush up their essential concepts. It is an important resource to quickly go through necessary concepts before the exams. Business Studies will help Class 12 students understand how businesses are crucial for the economy. In future, if students want to set up their own business then this subject will serve them beyond academics. NCERT Book for Class 12 Business Studies follows the syllabus prescribed by CBSE Board.
