Book Name: Science

Author: TN Government

About:

TN Board text books for Class 8 Science covers all the important topics and concepts that are prescribed in TN State board Curriculum and TN Board Class 8 Science Syllabus. All the topics and concepts are explained in easily understandable language. Class 8 Science Books Helps to build strong foundation on all the concepts. Most of the questions asked in the board examination are completely from the Class 8 Science TN SSLC Solutions.
